organize your next event with event.cntxt

Streamline Check-In

When guests arrive at your event, the event.cntxt mobile app will automatically check them in when they get within range of the check-in beacon. After a quick verification by an event attendant, guests are checked in and on their way in record timing.

Gather Live Data

As people attend your event, beacons gather data about popular sites throughout the event. View this data in easy to read charts on the event.cntxt web app to better understand your guest's needs and plan for your next event.

Keep Everyone Up to Date

Need to let everyone know something quickly? Push notifications through the event.cntxt mobile app to keep guests up to the second on every detail.

Simplify Transportation

The day of the event, users can request a Lyft within the event.cntxt app to take them exactly where they need to go. No more parking issues, no more confusing directions.

How it Works

You've spent all this time planning a great event, but it's time to make it better.

  1. Register your event and your Estimote beacons with event.cntxt at event.cntxt.com.
  2. Put the event.cntxt link we provide you with on your website and flyers for guests to register at.
  3. After guests register, we will send them a link to download the event.cntxt mobile app.
  4. Once they sign into the app, your event will appear in their Upcoming Events.
  1. On the day of your event, set up your beacons at check-in and any other sites where you want to track foot-traffic.
  2. Log onto your event.cntxt.com account and indicate which beacons you have set up at which site.
  3. As guests arrive, event.cntxt will check people in, track attendance, and record other data about your event.
  4. During or after your event, log onto your event.cntxt.com account to view the data we collected from your event!
